About the Game

This game is meant to be an educational game that would ideally be placed in a museum setting so that it will occupy the artistic space. Although the theme and story of the game is not necessarily directly related to art, its generative art within the game shows that the interaction can be an art form in itself. The goal is to educate as well as be a generative art piece that you, as a player can interact with.

OH NO!

An explosion has occurred on a drilling rig, causing the rapid spilling of oil into the surrounding bodies of water. The oil is causing intense damage to the ocean ecosystem in the surrounding area and is rapidly spreading. Luckily, a group of scientists have come up with a solution that will help with cleaning the disaster: Super Bacteria. You see, not all bacteria is wants to make you sick and some actually want to help keep the world into balance. Super Bacteria likes to break down oil in water. Help your own Super Bacteria clean up the ocean and save the wildlife!
